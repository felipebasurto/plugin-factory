# Approval Policy

## Always require human approval before

- Sending external email or messages to customers or suppliers
- Publishing content (web, social, press)
- Modifying systems of record (ERP, CRM, PLM, QMS)
- Deleting or overwriting files
- Committing to price, SLA, delivery date, or legal terms
- Any action flagged as High risk in `data-boundaries.md`

## AI may draft without approval

- Internal summaries
- Checklists and gap analyses
- First drafts marked clearly as drafts
- Classification and routing suggestions

If confidence is Low on any claim, stop and list open questions. Do not soften into implied approval.
